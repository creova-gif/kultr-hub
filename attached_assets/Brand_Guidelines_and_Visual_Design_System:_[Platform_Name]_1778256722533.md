## 5. Typography

We will use a combination of a modern sans-serif for headings and a highly readable sans-serif for body text, ensuring legibility across all screen sizes and devices. The fonts will support a wide range of East African languages.

**Primary Font (Headings & Display):** Montserrat (or similar modern, geometric sans-serif)
*   **Usage:** Bold, impactful headlines, feature titles, key numerical data.
*   **Weights:** Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600)

**Secondary Font (Body Text & UI Elements):** Open Sans (or similar humanist sans-serif)
*   **Usage:** Body paragraphs, UI labels, buttons, forms, captions.
*   **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600)

## 9. Creative Direction: Cinematic Motion & Immersive Interactions

Inspired by tools like Seedance, our platform will leverage motion design to create a truly immersive and engaging experience. Animations will be purposeful, smooth, and contribute to visual storytelling.

**Principles:**
*   **Purposeful Motion:** Every animation should serve a clear purpose, guiding the user, providing feedback, or enhancing understanding.
*   **Smooth Transitions:** Seamless transitions between screens and states to create a fluid and intuitive user journey.
*   **Emotional Engagement:** Animations will evoke a sense of delight, excitement, and cultural connection.
*   **Performance Optimized:** All animations will be optimized for smooth performance on mobile devices, even with varying network conditions.

**Examples of Motion & Interaction:**
*   **Hero Section:** Subtle parallax scrolling effects on event banners.
*   **Event Card Interactions:** Hover/tap effects that reveal more information with a smooth animation.
*   **Navigation Transitions:** Custom, branded transitions between main navigation sections.
*   **Loading States:** Unique, culturally inspired loading animations.
*   **Feedback Animations:** Delightful micro-interactions for likes, shares, and purchases.
*   **Video Previews:** Autoplay video previews for events with cinematic fades and overlays.

## 11. Design Principles for Consistency (Web & Mobile)

*   **Mobile-First:** Design and develop for mobile devices first, then scale up to larger screens.
*   **Atomic Design:** Build from smallest components to larger templates for consistency and reusability.
*   **Accessibility:** Ensure all designs meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards, with clear contrast, readable text, and navigable interfaces.
*   **Performance:** Prioritize fast loading times and smooth interactions.
*   **Cultural Relevance:** Integrate African design elements, patterns, and storytelling where appropriate, without compromising usability.
*   **Clarity & Simplicity:** Keep interfaces clean, uncluttered, and easy to understand.

## 12. Premium Onboarding & User Journey

Our onboarding experience will be highly engaging, personalized, and culturally immersive, designed to quickly introduce users to the platform's value proposition.

**Onboarding Flow:**
1.  **Welcome & Brand Story:** Short, animated introduction to the platform's vision and cultural roots.
2.  **Personalization:** Quick questions about interests, location, and language preferences to tailor event recommendations.
3.  **Permissions:** Clear and concise requests for necessary permissions (e.g., location, notifications).
4.  **Feature Highlights:** Interactive mini-tutorials or animated guides for key features.
5.  **First Event Discovery:** Immediately present personalized event recommendations.

**User Journey Touchpoints:**
*   **Event Discovery:** Intuitive search, filtering, and personalized recommendations.
*   **Event Details:** Rich media (videos, high-res images), clear pricing, location, and social sharing.
*   **Ticketing & Purchase:** Seamless, secure, and multi-currency payment flow.
*   **Post-Event Engagement:** Feedback, photo/video sharing, future recommendations.
*   **Merchant/Creator Onboarding:** Streamlined process for event organizers and vendors to list their offerings.
